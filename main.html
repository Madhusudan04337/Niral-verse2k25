WE NIRAL-VERSE

Niral-Verse 2025: The Ultimate Tech Fest of Hindustan College of Arts & Science Niralverse 2025 is the prestigious annual tech fest organized by the Department of Computer Applications at Hindustan College of Arts & Science. Niralverse is a highly anticipated event that unites students, innovators, and tech enthusiasts to explore cutting-edge ideas, showcase their expertise, and engage in exciting competitions. The fest features a dynamic mix of technical and creative events, challenging participants to demonstrate their skills, innovation, and problem-solving abilities. Known for its electrifying atmosphere and top-tier competitions, Niral-verse 2025 provides a thrilling platform to celebrate technology, knowledge, and excellence.
